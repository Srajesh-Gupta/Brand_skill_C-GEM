# C-GEM Programmes Reference

All programme names carry ™ always. Accent colours from `references/tokens.md`.

---

## Carbon Ready™
**Accent:** `--harvest #C98A1F`
**What it does:** Feasibility reports and climate portfolios for grassroots organisations. Helps community groups assess their land, understand carbon methodology options, and build a documented case for entering the market on their own terms.
**Copy angle:** Preparation, not dependence. Communities arrive at the table knowing what they have.
**Audience:** FPOs, grassroots NGOs, village-level institutions exploring carbon markets for the first time.

---

## Carbon Bridge™
**Accent:** `--ink #0E1410`
**What it does:** Connects vetted market actors (developers, buyers, standards bodies) with community projects — on terms the community helped set. C-GEM reviews and negotiates every contract. Takes no stake in the credits.
**Copy angle:** The only introduction in the market that has no financial interest in the outcome.
**Audience:** CSOs and FPOs ready to engage a market actor. Carbon developers and corporates seeking credible community partnerships.

---

## Climate Learn™
**Accent:** `--leaf-600 #2C7A3D`
**What it does:** Workshops, guidebooks, and training for farming communities, FPO staff, and partner organisations. Curriculum covers carbon markets, ecosystem services, monitoring & verification, rights, and negotiation skills.
**Copy angle:** Knowledge transfers power. A community that understands the methodology cannot be misled by it.
**Audience:** Farmer field schools, FPO boards, NGO field staff, state government agriculture departments.

---

## Beyond Carbon™
**Accent:** `--clay-600 #B85829`
**What it does:** Community stewardship programmes, new ecosystem service methodologies, and blended finance structures that go beyond carbon credits — biodiversity, water, soil health, livelihood. Long-horizon.
**Copy angle:** Carbon is one chapter. The land holds more, and communities should benefit from all of it.
**Audience:** Philanthropic funders, impact investors, government scheme integrators, biodiversity credit buyers.
